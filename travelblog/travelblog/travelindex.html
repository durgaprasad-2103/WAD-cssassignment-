Dubai Travel Itinerary

Your guide to an unforgettable adventure in Dubai

Day 1: Arrival and Evening Adventure

  • Morning: Arrive at Dubai International Airport and check in to your hotel.
  • Afternoon: Relax and enjoy the luxurious hotel amenities.
  • Evening: Visit the iconic Burj Khalifa and witness the mesmerizing Dubai Fountain show.

Day 2: Cultural Exploration and Shopping

  • Morning: Discover Dubai's heritage at the Al Fahidi District and explore the Dubai Museum.
  • Afternoon: Take a traditional abra ride across Dubai Creek and visit the Gold and Spice Souks.
  • Evening: Experience fine dining at Dubai Marina with stunning waterfront views.

Day 3: Desert Safari and Adventure

  • Morning: Relax on the serene Jumeirah Beach or enjoy a refreshing swim.
  • Afternoon: Embark on an exciting desert safari with activities like dune bashing and camel riding.
  • Evening: Savor a Bedouin-style dinner with cultural performances under the stars.
